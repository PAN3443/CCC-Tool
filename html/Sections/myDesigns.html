My Designs

?

The MyDesigns section stores your colormap specifications (CMS). You can fill this list with new created CMS or with copies of CMS from the Gallery section. Start with the generation of a CMS by clicking on the New CMS button or start with the selection of a predefined CMS by clicking on the Gallery button.

The figure below shows how the MyDesigns section presents you each of your CMS. You can edit a CMS with a click on the corresponding container. At the right top corner of a container are two buttons for the export of the CMS or to remove it from the MyDesigns list. The label at the left top corner gives you the information about the CMS name and the selected colorspace for the interpolation. (You can change the interpolation space of a CMS at the Edit section!)

For the visualization of the CMS, each container offers a linear colormap and a band sketch. The linear colormap shows the colormap with the right ratio of the key reference values. Unlike that, the band sketch uses equal widths for each band, which is the reference area between two keys. The band sketch version is well-suited to show you relatively small colormap parts, which could be skipped at the linear colormap visualization. (Please visit the CMS tutorial for more information about CMS, Bands, Keys, etc.)
